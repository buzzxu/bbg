# Hermes Strategy Adoption

## Scope

K12 records strategy adoption before outcome measurement.

## Guardrails

- Adoption decisions must reference accepted recommendations.
- Outcome verdicts must be evidence-backed and reviewable.
- K12 does not auto-apply or auto-roll back strategies.
