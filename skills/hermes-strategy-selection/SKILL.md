# Hermes Strategy Selection

## Workflow

1. Review proposed recommendations with linked evidence and signal confidence.
2. Validate consistency with canonical routing, trust, and promotion boundaries.
3. Decide accepted/rejected/superseded outcomes with rationale.
4. Record decision actor and timestamp for auditability.
5. Route accepted decisions into explicit downstream implementation workflows.

## Rules

- strategy changes require explicit human approval
- Decisions must preserve canonical-first and evidence-first governance boundaries
- Strategy decisions do not auto-apply edits or promotions
- K12 handles adoption rollout and outcome tracking after K11 decisions
