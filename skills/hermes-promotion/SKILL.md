# Hermes Promotion

## Workflow

1. Load candidates that have completed verification with auditable evidence.
2. Confirm target scope and policy constraints for each promotion decision.
3. Record approved, rejected, or deferred outcomes with rationale.
4. Preserve decision lineage back to intake and verification records.
5. Handoff approved decisions to explicit downstream publication workflows.

## Rules

- promotion decisions must reference verification results
- Approved/rejected/deferred outcomes must include rationale
- K10 promotion records do not include K11 meta-learning
