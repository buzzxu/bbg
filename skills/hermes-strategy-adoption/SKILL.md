# Hermes Strategy Adoption

## Workflow

1. Select accepted K11 recommendation records for rollout.
2. Define adoption scope, target, and rollout notes.
3. Record adoption status changes with timestamps and owner attribution.
4. Preserve linkages to upstream recommendations and downstream outcomes.
5. Escalate regressions or incidents to explicit review workflows.

## Rules

- adoption requires explicit linkage to accepted recommendations
- Strategy rollout updates must remain auditable and reversible
- K12 adoption records do not auto-edit code, rules, or templates
